Let's make a game!

*game
name:Infinite Expedition
by:You
desc:Start alone. Discover your purpose.

Resources


*energy
	name:Energy

*food
	name:Food

*materials
	name:Materials

*knowledge
	name:Knowledge

*exploration
	name:Exploration

*people
	name:Colonists

*tools
	name:Tools

*sticks
	name:Sticks

*stone
	name:Stone


Buttons


*exploreStart
	name:Explore
	desc:Look around.
	on click:yield 1 exploration
	on click:log You search the area...

*searchFood
	name:Search for Food
	on click:yield 1 food
	req:exploration>2

*gatherSticks
	name:Gather Sticks
	on click:yield 1 sticks
	req:exploration>1

*gatherStone
	name:Gather Stone
	on click:yield 1 stone
	req:exploration>2

*rest
	name:Rest
	on click:yield 2 energy

*discoverFire
	name:Discover Fire
	on click:yield 1 knowledge
	req:sticks>10 and stone>5

*unlockCivilization
	name:Settle Land
	on click:yield 1 people
	on click:log You begin building something bigger...
	req:knowledge>5

*work
	name:Work
	on click:yield 1 energy
	req:people>0

*forage
	name:Forage
	on click:yield 1 food
	req:energy>5 and people>0

*gatherMaterials
	name:Gather Materials
	on click:yield 1 materials
	req:energy>10 and people>0

*craftTools
	name:Craft Tools
	on click:yield 1 tools
	req:materials>10

*study
	name:Study
	on click:yield 1 knowledge
	req:tools>5

*exploreWorld
	name:Explore World
	on click:yield 1 exploration
	req:knowledge>10


Buildings


*camp
	name:Camp
	cost:10 sticks
	on tick:yield 1 energy

*farm
	name:Farms
	cost:20 materials
	on tick:yield 1 food

*workshop
	name:Workshop
	cost:30 materials
	on tick:yield 1 tools

*school
	name:School
	cost:50 materials
	on tick:yield 1 knowledge


Upgrades


*fireMaking
	name:Fire Mastery
	cost:10 sticks
	passive:multiply yield of energy by 2

*toolMaking
	name:Better Tools
	cost:20 tools
	passive:multiply yield of materials by 2

*education
	name:Education
	cost:50 knowledge
	passive:multiply yield of knowledge by 2


Achievements


*start
	name:First Step
	req:exploration>1

*food10
	name:Survivor
	req:food>10

*tools10
	name:Crafter
	req:tools>10

*knowledge10
	name:Thinker
	req:knowledge>10
