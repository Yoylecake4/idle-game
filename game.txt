Yeah I see the issue instantly — this one’s actually a syntax rule in IGM, not your logic.

❌ THE PROBLEM

IGM does NOT allow multiple on click: lines like this:

on click:yield 1 exploration
on click:yield 1 warUnlock

👉 It thinks you're starting a new effect inside another one → error.

✅ THE FIX (IMPORTANT)

You must combine them into ONE on click: block:

on click:
yield 1 exploration
yield 1 warUnlock
🧠 WHY THIS HAPPENS

IGM parsing works like:

on click: = start of block
everything after = part of SAME block
another on click: = ❌ illegal nested block
